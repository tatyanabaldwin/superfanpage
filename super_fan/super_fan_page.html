All About My Future Husband: Timothée Chalamet

Timothée Hal Chalamet is a French-American actor. He has stared in many films including Lady Bird and Dune.

Biography

He was born on December 27, 1995 and grew up in New York City. He grew up with is older sister Pauline Chalamet who is about 4 years older than him, his mother Nicole Flender, and his father Marc Chalamet. His sister Pauline is also and actress. Timothée's career in film started when he was still a child; he starred in Law and Order along with several commercials. His breakthrough role was in 2017 when he starred in the hit movie Call Me By Your Name as the lead.
